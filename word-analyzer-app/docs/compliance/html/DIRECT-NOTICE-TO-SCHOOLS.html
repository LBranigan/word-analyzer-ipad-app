About Word Analyzer

Word Analyzer is an iPad-based reading assessment tool that helps educators:

  • Record students reading aloud
  • Capture text from reading materials
  • Analyze pronunciation accuracy and fluency
  • Track student reading progress over time
  • Generate assessment reports and videos
  • Provide personalized AI-generated feedback with voice playback

How Information Is Used

Student information is used exclusively for:

  1. Analyzing reading pronunciation and accuracy
  2. Tracking reading progress over time
  3. Generating assessment reports for educators
  4. Identifying reading error patterns
  5. Providing personalized AI feedback to encourage students
We do NOT use student information for:
  • Advertising or marketing of any kind
  • Creating behavioral or commercial profiles
  • Selling or renting to any third party
  • Training AI models
  • Any purpose unrelated to educational assessment

Third Parties Who Receive Information

To provide the App's functionality, we share limited information with Google Cloud services only. Using a single vendor simplifies compliance as most schools already have Google Cloud Data Processing Agreements in place.

Service What is Shared Why Retention
Google Cloud Speech-to-Text Audio recordings Transcribe speech Not stored
Google Cloud Vision Images of text Extract text (OCR) Not stored
Google Cloud Text-to-Speech AI summary (incl. first name) Generate voice feedback Not stored
Google Gemini AI First name + metrics Generate personalized summary Not used for training
Firebase (Google) All assessment data Secure storage Until deletion requested
Important Notes:
  • All services are provided by Google Cloud (single vendor)
  • Audio recordings and images are stored only 24 hours, then automatically deleted
  • Only text-based assessment results are retained long-term
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption

School's Responsibilities

If your school consents on behalf of parents, you agree to:

  • Notify Parents - Inform parents that the school has authorized the use of Word Analyzer
  • Provide Information - Make this notice and our Privacy Policy available to parents upon request
  • Honor Parent Requests - Forward any parent requests to access, review, or delete their child's information to us
  • Limit Use - Ensure the App is used only for educational purposes

Parent Rights

Google Cloud DPA

Most schools already have a Google Cloud or Google Workspace Data Processing Agreement in place. Word Analyzer uses exclusively Google Cloud services, which means:

  • Your existing Google DPA likely covers Word Analyzer's data processing
  • No additional third-party agreements are needed
  • Data stays within Google's infrastructure

Please verify your school's Google Cloud DPA coverage with your IT administrator.
