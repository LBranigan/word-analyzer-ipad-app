Parent Rights Notice

Word Analyzer Reading Assessment App
For Parents and Guardians

? What is Word Analyzer?

Word Analyzer is an iPad app that helps teachers assess how well your child reads aloud. Here's how it works:

1
Your child reads a passage out loud
2
The app records the audio
3
The app takes a picture of the text
4
The app checks which words were correct
5
Your child gets an encouraging message
6
Teacher tracks reading progress

i What Information is Collected?

About Your Child

Information How It's Used
Name So the teacher knows whose reading it is
Grade Level To compare with typical reading levels
Voice Recording To check pronunciation and fluency
Reading Results Accuracy, speed, and areas to practice
What We DON'T Collect:
  • No photos of your child (only photos of the text they're reading)
  • No location information
  • No information for advertising
  • No social media connections

~ How Long is Information Kept?

Type of Data How Long
Voice recordings Deleted after 24 hours
Pictures of text Deleted after 24 hours
Reading scores Kept until you or the school asks us to delete it

@ Who Sees My Child's Information?

Your Child's Teacher

  • Can see all reading results
  • Uses the information to help your child improve

Google Cloud Services

Word Analyzer uses Google's cloud services to process the audio and images. This is the same Google that many schools already use.

  • Google's computers analyze the recording to figure out what words were said
  • Google does NOT keep the audio or images
  • Google does NOT use your child's information for advertising

* Your Rights as a Parent

You have the right to:

1 See Your Child's Information

Ask to see what reading results have been collected about your child.

2 Delete Your Child's Information

Ask us to delete all information about your child. We'll do it within 30 days.

3 Stop Collection

Tell us not to collect any more information. (Note: Your child won't be able to use the app anymore.)

4 Ask Questions

Contact us anytime with questions about your child's privacy.

? Why Schools Can Consent for Parents

Under federal law (COPPA), schools are allowed to consent to the collection of student information on behalf of parents when:

  • The information is used only for education
  • The information is not used for advertising
  • Parents are told about the app

Your school has agreed to notify parents (like through this notice) that Word Analyzer is being used.
