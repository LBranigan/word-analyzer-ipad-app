## About Word Analyzer

Word Analyzer is an iPad-based reading assessment tool that helps educators:
- Record students reading aloud
- Capture text from reading materials
- Analyze pronunciation accuracy and fluency
- Track student reading progress over time
- Generate assessment reports and videos
- Provide personalized AI-generated feedback with voice playback

## Personal Information We Collect

### From Students Directly

| Information | Collection Method | Purpose |
|-------------|-------------------|---------|
| **Audio Recording** | Device microphone | Analyze reading accuracy and fluency |
| **Images of Text** | Device camera | Extract text for comparison |

### Entered by Teachers/School Staff

| Information | Purpose |
|-------------|---------|
| **Student Name** | Identify student for progress tracking |

### Generated by the App

| Information | Purpose |
|-------------|---------|
| **Assessment Scores** | Measure reading accuracy |
| **Error Patterns** | Identify areas for improvement |
| **Reading Speed (WPM)** | Measure fluency |
| **Prosody Score** | Assess reading expression |
| **AI Summary** | Personalized feedback for student |
| **Audio Feedback** | Spoken version of AI summary |

---

## How Information Is Used

Student information is used **exclusively** for:

1. Analyzing reading pronunciation and accuracy
2. Tracking reading progress over time
3. Generating assessment reports for educators
4. Identifying reading error patterns
5. Providing personalized AI feedback to encourage students

**We do NOT use student information for:**
- Advertising or marketing of any kind
- Creating behavioral or commercial profiles
- Selling or renting to any third party
- Training AI models
- Any purpose unrelated to educational assessment

## Third Parties Who Receive Information

To provide the App's functionality, we share limited information with **Google Cloud services only**. Using a single vendor simplifies compliance as most schools already have Google Cloud Data Processing Agreements in place.

### Google Cloud Speech-to-Text API
| What is Shared | Why | Retention |
|----------------|-----|-----------|
| Audio recordings | Transcribe speech | Not stored - processed and discarded |

### Google Cloud Vision API
| What is Shared | Why | Retention |
|----------------|-----|-----------|
| Images of reading material | Extract text (OCR) | Not stored - processed and discarded |

### Google Cloud Text-to-Speech API
| What is Shared | Why | Retention |
|----------------|-----|-----------|
| AI summary text (includes first name) | Generate spoken feedback | Not stored - processed and discarded |

### Google Gemini AI
| What is Shared | Why | Retention |
|----------------|-----|-----------|
| First name + assessment metrics | Generate personalized summary | API calls not used for training |

### Firebase (Google Cloud)
| What is Shared | Why | Retention |
|----------------|-----|-----------|
| Student names, grades, assessment results | Secure storage | Until deletion requested |
| Audio, images, videos | Temporary storage | 24 hours then auto-deleted |

**Important Notes:**
- All services are provided by Google Cloud (single vendor)
- Audio recordings and images are stored only 24 hours, then automatically deleted
- Only text-based assessment results are retained long-term
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Data is isolated by school/teacher account
- Google API calls are not used for model training

## FERPA Compliance

Under FERPA, Word Analyzer may be designated as a "school official" with legitimate educational interest, allowing the school to share education records without additional parent consent, provided:

1. Word Analyzer performs institutional services or functions
2. Word Analyzer has a legitimate educational interest in the records
3. Word Analyzer is under the direct control of the school with respect to use of records
4. Word Analyzer complies with FERPA's restrictions on re-disclosure

We agree to all of these conditions.
